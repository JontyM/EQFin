RISK PRODUCTS

Life Insurance
Death is unpredictable and with all your responsibilities, it is a necessity to have life insurance. Liberty's insurance protects your loved ones from financial strain when you die and has the following benefits:

  • Premiums based on your personal details
  • You can increase or decrease your cover to suit your changing circumstances

Disability Cover
Liberty's disability cover covers you if anything were to happen to you that would leave you physically disabled or unable to work as you used to in your area of expertise and/or qualification. With Liberty's Disability Cover you get:

  • A lump sum cash payout if you become permanently disabled and are unable to work
  • To protect your family and dependants from unexpected financial crisis
  • To support your children through school even after losing your salary due to becoming disabled

Income Protection
If you were to be retrenched, fall ill or have an accident that leaves you unable to work temporarily, Liberty's income protection is your safety cushion. Liberty's income protection will pay you your salary for up to 6 months after such instances. However, if you become ill or disabled, you will receive income payouts until the age of 65.

Dread Disease
Liberty's dread disease cover pays out a lump sum if you are diagnosed with one of the 31 specified conditions. However, Liberty's cover can be tailor-made to your needs determined by the severity and level of the critical illness based on the benefit categories and payment criteria.

Funeral Benefits
Liberty's funeral benefits cover you and your family when the death of a loved one occurs.
The benefits of our funeral plan are:

  • Minimum administration
  • Claims attended to within 24 hours
  • Complete peace of mind to members

RETIREMENT PLANNING

Retirement Annuities
Retirement annuities are the ideal way to save for your retirement. You would make chosen monthly contributions until you reach retirement age, which can be from the age of 55.

Living Annuities
A life annuity is a policy that pays a guaranteed income until the day of your death. Life annuities are particularly suitable if you have no other material source of income or capital. Liberty's life annuity will suit most retirees but there are circumstances where a living annuity may be more appropriate. To find these out, please contact your financial advisor.

Tax Planning for Retirees
Retirees often receive income from a variety of sources, including distributions from pensions, annuities, RAs and other retirement plans. EQ.FIN provides your tax planning when you retire.

INVESTMENT MANAGEMENT

EQ.FIN can provide both individuals and corporates with access to a premier range of portfolio management services and products.

Unit Trusts
You should consider putting part of your portfolio into unit trusts if you do not have the expertise, time or money to invest in the stock market but still want exposure to shares.

LISP (Linked Investment Service Provider)

As the client, you are enabled to invest in a wide range of collective investment schemes, such as unit trust funds, via one source. A LISP also offers access to traditional life insurance products such as endowments, retirement annuities, preservation funds and living annuities to cater for the full range of your investment needs.
